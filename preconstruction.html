What pre-construction covers

Conceptual & Detailed Estimating

From an early order-of-magnitude number to a line-item budget — so you know what you're really building before you fund it.

Value Engineering

We find the cost without cutting the quality — smarter assemblies, better sequencing, and material choices that protect your number and your finish.

Constructability & Design Review

We read the set the way the field will. Conflicts, gaps, and buildability problems get caught on paper, where they cost a redline instead of a change order.

Scheduling & Phasing

A realistic, honest schedule — including how to phase around an occupied site, tenant, or opening date.

Permitting & Entitlement Strategy

With deep zoning and land-development experience in-house, we map the permitting and entitlement path early — so approvals don't blindside your timeline.

Why it pays for itself

Every dollar spent planning saves several in the field. Pre-construction turns a hopeful budget into a defensible one, and a wished-for schedule into a buildable one — before you've committed real capital.
