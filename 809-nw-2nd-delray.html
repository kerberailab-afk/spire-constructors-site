The Project

A clean modern build on a Downtown Delray lot.

On a tight 0.11-acre infill lot in the heart of Downtown Delray Beach, Spire is building a custom two-story modern residence from the ground up. The design is unmistakably contemporary — flat rooflines, floor-to-ceiling glass, warm wood cladding, and a crisp white stucco envelope set behind a private wall.

Inside the 2,390 square feet under air: an open living-kitchen core, a covered loggia, four bedrooms, a second-floor loft, and a front balcony — wrapped around a private pool and an attached garage. A complete, ground-up custom home, self-managed by Spire as general contractor.

  • Ground-up new construction
  • Site clearing & foundations
  • Structural & framing
  • Full MEP — electrical, plumbing, HVAC
  • Stucco envelope & wood cladding
  • Impact glass & modern glazing
  • Private pool & hardscape
  • Custom interior finishes
  • Landscaping & site walls
