The Project

A statement home in the heart of Delray.

On a deep infill lot on SW 2nd Avenue — just around the corner from Sundy Village, the crown jewel of Delray's current downtown renaissance — Spire is building a three-story modern single-family residence with a detached rear accessory dwelling.

The design leans into a warm, modern material palette: crisp white stucco, wood-clad accents, vertical louvers, and rooftop greenery. The main residence runs roughly 3,188 square feet across three levels — about 1,219 on the first floor, 1,198 on the second, and 771 up top — crowned by a rooftop entertainment lounge. Add the detached ~883 SF ADU and the project totals just over 4,071 square feet of finished structure on a 7,841 SF lot. A complete ground-up build — main house and ADU — self-managed by Spire as general contractor.
