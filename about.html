About Spire

Built by builders who've been there.

Spire Constructors was founded by partners Bill and Gary, who bring 50+ years of combined leadership experience and a track record of 100+ projects and $250M delivered across commercial and residential construction.

We started Spire to fix what frustrated us most.

Opaque pricing, endless change orders, and clients left in the dark. Our model is the opposite — transparent, communicative, and relentlessly hands-on. Senior partners stay involved from the first estimate to the final walkthrough, on every single project.

Transparency

Honest pricing and open books. No surprise change orders.

Communication

Daily logs, real-time updates, and a client portal on every job.

Attention

Technology

Time-lapse and drone documentation on every new build.
