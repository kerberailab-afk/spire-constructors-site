The Brief

Don't blend in. Outperform.

Our client is an investor — and his goal was simple to say and hard to deliver: a Delray Beach short-term rental that would stand out above the rest and generate real, reliable cash flow. In a market full of look-alike rentals, "nice" isn't enough. It has to be the listing people scroll back up to book.

So we treated the build like a product. Spire took the property end-to-end — a full transformation of the home, a resort-style backyard with a custom pool, bold large-scale murals, and a clean, photo-ready coastal interior. Every decision was made through one lens: will this drive bookings?

  • Full interior transformation
  • Custom in-ground pool
  • Resort backyard & pergola lounge
  • Large-scale artist murals
  • Color-changing pool & landscape lighting
  • Designer kitchen & baths
  • Turf, landscaping & outdoor games
  • Turnkey, listing-ready handover
